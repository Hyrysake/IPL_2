Future landing page of IPL solution

Today, Lutsk (like other cities in Ukraine and Europe) is overloaded with cars. On one hand, this creates a burden on roads, but it also creates no less chaos in parking lots. Parking near a school is Brownian motion, where parents try to find a place at least to stop, while some spaces are occupied by cars unrelated to the school. Near business centers, 'guest cars' often occupy spaces of permanent tenants, and the latter have to look for spaces somewhere further away.

When we learned about photo recognition and databases, we thought that this could actually be a good idea that would provide an opportunity to effectively bring order to parking lots. This is how the idea of IPL – Intelligent Parking Lot came about.

Intelligent Parking Lot is an innovative students’ startup. We are just starting with this idea, but if you want to learn more, don’t hesitate to contact us.
